About Me

I am Jeonghaeng Lee, a Ph.D. candidate at Yonsei University, supervised by Prof. Sanghoon Lee. My current research topic is virtual reality and AI-driven multimedia applications. Specifically, I develop various multimedia content creation technologies based on how humans perceive the world, and through this, I aim to create 4D human avatars that can be used across entertainment industry fields.
